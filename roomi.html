Bunki

I was lucky enough to be given the oppurtunity to work with a startup called Roomi. Roomi is a free web and mobile platform that helps people find affordable co-living solutions through a peer-peer marketplace. Together with a small team we were to design and implement a standalone tool which would solve the questions shared by many: "where should I live and who should I live with?" We decided to create a website where we would gather answers from the user and plug those answers into an algorithmn to find the perfect roommate within Roomi's listings.
