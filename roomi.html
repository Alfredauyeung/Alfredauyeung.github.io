Bunki

Assignment

I was lucky enough to be given the opportunity to work with a startup called Roomi. Together with a small team we had 6 weeks to create a tool to solve: "where should I live and who should I live with?" We decided to create Bunki, a website where the user takes a quiz and with the answers gathered plug it into an algorithm to find the perfect location and roommate within Roomi's listings.

Process

  • Research the neighborhoods Roomi was active in.
  • Create a quiz based off of the research.
  • Make the wireframes and high fidelity
    mock-ups.
  • Begin coding the site using Bootstrap as our framework.
  • Connecting the front-end and back-end to give the user their perfect location and roommate.
  • Lastly implementing a marketing plan to promote Bunki.

Takeaways

  • Getting an understanding of how back-end and front-end developers work together.
  • Gaining knowledge on responsive design through the Bootstrap framework.
  • Working on a tight deadline to create a functioning product.
  • Implementing a marketing strategy to get the product out to the general public.
