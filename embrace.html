Embrace Tutoring

Out of college and hungry to grow as a designer I began my journey as a freelancer, taking jobs that I knew would help me grow. I was approached by a friend's fraternity brother who was in the process of starting a tutoring company based in New Jersey. Their mission was very simple, to create the best educational experience for their clients by providing them with top-level teachers and mentors that are experienced classroom teachers. Having had many tutors in the past, that I felt were never helpful, their mission really resonated with me.He needed a designer to help create the branding for Embrace Tutoring and I immediately jumped on this opportunity. I was brought on board and created their logo, which went through many changes during the process, as well as their business cards.

After proven success in the initial branding of the company, I was asked to assume all marketing duties and helped design their original website. Over the past couple of years working with Embrace Tutoring I have created flyers, postcards, merchandise and helped with the development of different marketing strategies. The journey with Embrace Tutoring has been one of the best work experiences I have had, even though it got bumpy at times we were able to overcome these bumps and grow together.
