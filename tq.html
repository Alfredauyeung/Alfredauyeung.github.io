Tetra Quest

The Start

I have always had a passion for game development and design, everything about the whole process of how a game comes to life was both perplexing and intriguing. This definitely came from being a gamer most of my life. So to pursue this passion I decided to develop and build a game with a close friend who was in the process of learning Unity, thus Tetra Quest was born.

Process

  • Figuring out how to mix Tetris and a role playing aspect to create a new entity.
  • Developing the process of how the game works which included: the players objective, battle sequence, leveling and class system, gear collection, the campaign and much more.
  • Creating the flow chart and wire frames for the mobile application.
  • Begin creating the UI/UX elements of the game and the style we want to implement.
  • Designing different sprites for the monsters, gears, skill effects, unique Tetris block skins, etc.
  • Creating a MVP in Unity and having people test the game
  • Figuring out how to potentially make revenue as well as market the application.

Takeaways

  • Learning about the steps of how to develop and create a game.
  • Gaining knowledge on new software.
  • Understanding the importance of UI/UX design within game development.
  • Practicing my illustration skills and diving deep into a new art style.
  • Learning more about the gamification process.
  • As this is an ongoing project I know there will be many more takeaways to come.
